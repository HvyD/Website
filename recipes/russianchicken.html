Gramcracker's Russian Chicken

A Family Classic

Posted by Karin M. Davis on April 19, 2020

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 15 ounce bottle Russian or Catalina Salad Dressing
  • 1 packet Onion Soup Mix

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 ℉/205 ℃
  2. Pour Dressing into square glass baking dish.
  3. Empty contents of Soup Mix into dressing and stir until throughly mixed.
  4. Slice chicken into tender sized bits.
  5. Add chicken to baking dish, coating all sides with sauce.
  6. Cover dish with foil and bake 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked to 165 ℉/75 ℃.
  7. Serve over cooked rice or with bread.
